Title: Flatmates Bill

Description: An app that gets an input as the amount of a bill for a particular period and the days that each of the flatmates stayed in the house for that period and returns how much each flatmates has to pay. It also generates a PDF report stating the names of the flatmates, the period, and how much each of them had to pay.

Objects: Bill:
            amount
            period
         Flatmate:
             name
             days_in_house
             pays(days_in_house, bill)

         PdfReport:
         filename
         .generate(flatmate1, flatmate2, bill)
